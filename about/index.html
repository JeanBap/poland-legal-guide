About Poland Legal Guide

An independent directory of verified English-speaking law firms in Poland for expats, foreign investors and international businesses.

Our Mission

Poland Legal Guide exists because finding an English-speaking lawyer in Poland is genuinely difficult for someone who has just arrived in the country or is planning to invest from abroad. International directories exist but are expensive for small firms to participate in; local bar association registers are in Polish and do not identify English-language capability.

We have compiled this directory by combining data from Chambers Europe, Legal 500 EMEA, Forbes Best Law Firms Poland and Who's Who Legal with our own research into language capability. Every firm on this site has been checked for English-language service.

Ranking Methodology

Firms are ranked within each practice area according to the following criteria, in descending order of weight:

  1. Chambers Europe band ranking (Band 1 weighted highest)
  2. Legal 500 EMEA tier ranking (Tier 1 weighted highest)
  3. Forbes Best Law Firms Poland inclusion
  4. Who's Who Legal recognition
  5. Verified English-language capability
  6. Volume of lawyers in the relevant practice area

No firm has paid for inclusion or ranking position. This is an independent editorial directory. We do not accept payment for rankings, sponsored placements or featured listings.

Data Currency

Firm data was last comprehensively reviewed in April 2026. Rankings are based on the most recently published Chambers Europe and Legal 500 EMEA editions (2025 editions, reflecting research conducted in 2024–2025). Contact details and firm profiles change — always verify current information on the firm's own website before making contact.
